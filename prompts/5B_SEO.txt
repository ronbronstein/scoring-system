2. STATUS SIGNAL CHECK:
   - Does sharing this content make the sharer look:
     - **Smart/Strategic?** (High Score).
     - **Helpful/Tactical?** (Mid Score).
     - **Novice/Basic?** (Low Score).

3. KEYWORD NATURALISM (SEO Hygiene):
   - Scan for "Keyword Stuffing" (Shoehorning).
   - **Fail:** "We offer the best CRM Strategy for your CRM Strategy needs when building a CRM Strategy." (Robotic repetition).
   - **Pass:** Keywords flow naturally within the sentence structure.
</evaluation_criteria>
