3. CONCRETE ACTION:
   - Compare "Process improvements" (Abstract) vs. "Automating the approval workflow" (Concrete).

3 (Publishable): Generally concrete. Avoids the word "thing." Uses specific nouns (e.g., "Sales pipeline" instead of "Process"). Some quantification is present.
4 (Exemplary): "Crystal Clear." Claims are quantified ("30% growth", "3 days saved"). Zero vague nouns. Replaces "many aspects" with specific lists.
