## Building Your Scoring Framework

The first step in the development of a lead scoring system is to conduct an analysis of your historical data. You need to give consideration to multiple dimensions of information:

**Demographic factors** - These are various elements related to company size, industry, job title, and other aspects that indicate fit. Really think about which things matter most for your specific situation.

**Behavioral factors** - This involves the evaluation of numerous components like email opens, website visits, content downloads, and various other engagement indicators. These aspects are actually very important for the prediction of intent.

**Firmographic factors** - Give consideration to different elements of the organization itself, including revenue, employee count, technology stack, and other substantial factors.

When you make a determination about scoring criteria, it's really important to carefully think through all the various things that could impact outcomes. The effective management of these numerous elements requires significant attention to detail.
