## More Metrics That Drive Results

**Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)** reveals what you're really spending to acquire each customer across various marketing and sales activities, and this metric matters for understanding unit economics. **Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)** shows the total revenue a customer generates, and comparing CLV to CAC creates insights into profitability across multiple dimensions. When the ratio drops below 3:1, you're experiencing problems that need really immediate attention. Platform users tracking these metrics in monday CRM see much better results because the visibility drives better decision-making across numerous aspects of the business.

**Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)** and **Annual Recurring Revenue (ARR)** are critical for subscription businesses and really need constant monitoring across various time periods. **Churn Rate** measures how many customers you're actually losing, and even small changes create substantial long-term impacts that affect growth trajectories in significant ways. **Quota Attainment** tracks what percentage of reps hit their targets, and low attainment suggests problems with territory design, compensation structure, or various other factors that need examination. Monday CRM makes tracking these things easy and will increase your team's performance substantially.

**Activity Metrics** including calls made, emails sent, meetings booked, and demos completed really matter because they're leading indicators that predict future results across multiple dimensions. The boss needs visibility into these various activities to actually understand if the team maintains proper discipline. **Forecast Accuracy** compares what you predicted to what actually closed, and improving this metric drives better business planning across numerous elements of the organization. Finally, **Time to Productivity** for new reps and **Deal Slippage** both reveal important aspects of sales effectiveness that impact results in really substantial ways.
