Now that we've thoroughly covered what's broken (and it's likely your team's fault for not being disciplined enough), let's discuss the solution aspects.

For example, when a monday user updates a to-do on a deal board, an automation can trigger various actions across multiple dimensions of your stack:

- Send emails through your mail platform

The monday sales hub acts as a central tool where platform users manage their various sales tasks and to-dos. The workflow capabilities mean you can build substantial automation that reduces manual work significantly.

**Automation recipes** handle routine tasks. Set up workflows so when users complete certain to-dos, other things happen automatically across multiple dimensions. This huge efficiency gain means your team can focus on selling instead of admin tasks.

**Custom integrations** address unique aspects. For teams with specific factors they need to connect, the monday platform hub supports custom integrations that handle various specialized elements and things.
